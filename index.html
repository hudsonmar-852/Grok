ChatGPT × Grok Imagine 影片提示詞工作台

使用 Summary

這個網站用來整理 Grok Imagine 影片 Prompt。你可以按不同影片用途選擇提示詞， 然後一鍵 Copy 到 Grok。設計重點是：角色一致、場景連貫、動作自然、短片有故事感。

1. 角色延續影片

適合由一張人物相片開始，生成自然連續短片。

Create a cinematic 8-second video using the uploaded character reference. Keep the same face identity, hairstyle, body proportion, outfit, and overall mood. The subject moves naturally within the same environment, with subtle facial expression changes, realistic breathing, and smooth body motion. Camera movement should be slow and cinematic, with no sudden scene change.

2. 故事延伸 Scene

適合做第 2、3、4 段延伸影片，避免突然轉場。

Continue directly from the previous scene. Maintain the same character, same location, same lighting, same emotional tone, and same cinematic style. Extend the action naturally as if this is the next 8-second part of the same story. Do not reset the scene. Do not introduce new characters unless already visible.

3. 情緒互動影片

適合人物之間有眼神、距離、表情變化。

Generate a realistic emotional interaction scene. The characters maintain natural eye contact, subtle facial reactions, small hand gestures, and believable body language. The mood should feel intimate, warm, cinematic, and human. Avoid exaggerated acting, avoid artificial expressions, and keep the motion realistic.

4. IG Reels 戲劇感

適合短片開頭有強烈畫面吸引力。

Create a highly engaging vertical cinematic video for Instagram Reels. Start with a strong visual hook in the first second. Use expressive body language, clear emotional contrast, and smooth camera movement. The scene should feel realistic, stylish, and instantly understandable without dialogue.

5. 超寫實人物鏡頭

適合提升真實感、去 AI 感。

Ultra-realistic cinematic portrait video, natural skin texture, realistic eyes, subtle imperfections, authentic facial movement, soft natural lighting, shallow depth of field, professional camera look, no plastic skin, no CGI feeling, no artificial smile, no distorted hands.

6. 影片收尾停格

適合結尾自然停住，不會亂延伸。

End the clip naturally once the main action is completed. The character should settle into a calm resting pose, with mouth movement stopped, relaxed facial expression, and stable body posture. Do not add extra actions after the scene objective is finished.
